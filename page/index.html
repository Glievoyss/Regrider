Regrider: A Wave Model of Light Propagation

Author: Serhii Hliievyi

Published: April 14, 2025

Zenodo DOI: https://zenodo.org/records/15211324

Regrider is a wave-based model of light propagation in a local moving medium. Unlike Special Relativity which assumes no medium, the Regrider model treats light as propagating at speed C within a localized, possibly moving structure â€” the regrider. This interpretation preserves all tested predictions of STR and adds physical intuition to wave behavior in motion.

Key Features

  • Constant local speed of light (C) within the regrider
  • Apparent variation in distance from external frames
  • Doppler effect explained via medium boundaries
  • Experimental predictions and testable implications
  • STR emerges as a special case (Sk = 0)
